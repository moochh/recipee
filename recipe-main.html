< Chocolate Hazelnut Fruit Crepes
  4.0/5.0
French
Breakfast
Pastry
Prep:
25 mins
Cook:
15 mins
Total:
20 mins
Servings:
4
Ingredients
1 cup chocolate hazelnut spread
 
4 (7 inch) pre-made crepes
 
4 bananas, sliced
 
1 (7 ounce) can pressurized whipped cream
Procedures
Step 1
Spread 1/4 cup of chocolate hazelnut spread onto each crepe.
Step 2
Arrange 1 sliced banana down the center of each one. Roll up, and place in a warm skillet over medium heat. Let them warm up for about 90 seconds.
Step 3
Transfer to plates, and serve topped with whipped cream.
About
Here is a simple and delicious treat featuring a thin, tender crepe stuffed with a creamy chocolate-hazelnut spread and bananas lightly sauteed in brown sugar and butter. It tastes elegant but takes so little effort.
 
 
Emeril Lagasse
